Best Food to Eat in the Sunday Morning

Ingredient Amount
Mamma Pancakes 400g
Alma Milk 0,5l
Strawberrys 200g
Honey 100g
Nutella 50g

  1. Heat up the pancakes

  2. Pour 1 class of milk

  3. Add some strawberries

  4. Add on top of the strawberries honey or Nutella

  5. Enjoy the Best Morning
